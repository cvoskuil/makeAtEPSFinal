Laser Cutter

Description

The Eastside Preparatory School is the wondurful academy that houses the EPS Maker Space where laser cutting is available. An example of one of the lasers is the zing laser which is The usable laser cutter in the EPS Make Lab. Smaller and slightly slower than the ULS Cutter. The ULS cutter is a slightly larger and less advanced cutter compared to the Zing Cutter.

Operation

Step 1: Make sure inkscape is Downloaded and create a new file.

Step 2: Paste in or draw desired image.

Step 3: Make border .001 inches thick with red lines.

Step 4: Check width of wood (in cm) and change this under Files/Document Properties.

Step 5: Save as PDF and use thumbdrive to get file to permanant Laser Cutter Computer.

Step 6: Turn on air system and air compressor for Laser Cutter, wait 45 seconds.

Step 7: Use program on LC computer to begin laser cutting.

Step 8: Wait for cut/etch to finish, might take a few minutes.

Step 9: Once done, just reach in and grab your cut.
