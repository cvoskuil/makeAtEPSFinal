Vinyl Cutter

Descrption

Create stunning sticker and t-shirts by using the vinyl cutter. It's as easy as designing and printing. To get started, just read the instructions and make sure to ask the staff any questions you have.

Types of Vinyl

Sticky Vinyl

T-Shirt Vinyl

Getting Started

Step 1: Create the document using Inkscape. 340 mm wide, 100 mm tall. Height may vary, width must stay the same.

Step 2: Trace bitmap by using the trace bitmap tool. Configure settings until image looks as you want it, black and white.

Step 3: Fill the remaining space on the page. Make sure stickers cover the entirety of the document.

Step 4: Save the document as a PDF. Do not resize once it is a PDF.

Step 5: Transfer the PDF to the vinyl cutting computer uisng a flash-drive or SD card.

Step 6: Check the print properties by hitting print. Select milimeters as units, length as 100, and rotate as off.

Step 7: Set vinyl cutter to edge and press enter.

Step 8: Press "get from machine" button once the vinyl cutter has found the edge.

Step 9: Press print once the width changes.

Finishing Steps

Step 1: Cut the vinyl off of the roll using a box cutter

Step 2: Peel the outside of the sticker using your fingers or a tweezer. Only your sticker should remain.

Step 3: Use the sticker! When you are ready, peel the sticker and place it on anything you want!
